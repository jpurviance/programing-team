Programming Team Offseason

Programming Team Offseason @ VT

Monday, Wednesday and Thursday @ 6 pm

Off-Season Practice

Meeting Info

Meetings every Monday, Wednesday and Thursday at 5 pm in NCB?

What to Expect

Coding problems focused on helping members increase problems solving skills and improve performance in coding competitions.

Food

Pizza and drinks will be provided at meetings. Come early to get a slice before there gone.

What You Need

You will need to bring your computer. Most members program in Python or Java but C, C++, Scala and more are supported. A notebook and pencil are also recommended.

Problem Sets

Topics will be taught be present and past Programming Team members. Concepts covered will include Big-O, Dynamic Programming, Graphs, Computational Geometry and more. Check out our full tentative list of topics. Don't see something that you would like to learn about? Contact Peter Steele: peter707@vt.edu

Partnering with Industry

Student Development

Virginia Tech Programming Team is a student group that is operated and managed by current and past members of the team. Our goal is to help our members become better coders, problem solvers, and engineers. Our current and past graduates are working at Amazon, Google, Facebook, Bloomberg and more...

Company Involvement

We hold company-sponsored competitions throughout the semester to help our members jumpstart their career after college. If you would like to become involved, please reach out to Peter Steele: peter707@vt.edu or John Purviance: jpurviance@vt.edu for more details. Companies normally bring representatives, provide refreshments, prizes as well as collect resumes.
